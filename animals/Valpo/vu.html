DRIVE POSITIVE CHANGE WITH AN MPA DEGREE.

Pursue your passion for public service and make a greater impact with Villanova’s Master of Public Administration (MPA). This newly online degree will prepare you to effectively lead across local/state/federal government, non-governmental organizations (NGO) and the non-profit sector. Accomplished instructors will develop your analytical, decision-making, management and leadership skills, providing you with the knowledge you need to enhance organizational change, develop programs and solve problems.

Experience The Villanova Difference

Founded in 1842, Villanova University has been providing academic excellence for generations. U.S. News & World Report has ranked Villanova as the #1 Regional University in the North – a distinction held for 20 years – and the MPA degree program is accredited by the National Association of Schools of Public Affairs and Administration (NASPAA). With convenient, 100% online learning, you can earn your degree in about two years, receiving the same high-quality education and degree as campus-based students through the following:

  • Instructor-led live, virtual classes through two-way online voice communication
  • Real-time chat rooms and discussion boards for networking and interaction
  • Video-based, MP3 and MP4 course lectures
  • Animations, simulations and interactions to maximize learning
